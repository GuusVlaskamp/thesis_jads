This dataset was exported via roboflow.ai on June 15, 2021 at 9:17 AM GMT

It includes 790 images.
Logotest3 are annotated in Tensorflow TFRecord (raccoon) format.

The following pre-processing was applied to each image:
* Auto-orientation of pixel data (with EXIF-orientation stripping)
* Resize to 1280x704 (Fill (with center crop))

No image augmentation techniques were applied.
